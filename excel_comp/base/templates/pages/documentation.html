Overview

Quelea2 Excel Compare is an online platform for comparing Excel document. It accepts pair of documents and returns a comparison viewer with the files displayed side by side. The comparison viewer is embeddable, and displays a side-by-side or vertical comparison of the documents. The documents are displayed in their original format, with changes highlighted.

Getting Started

To get started, log in or create an account. If you have a question or need help, contact us or email us at support@quelea2.com

Once logged in, select the option to compare and you will be greeted with the comparison page.

Comparisons

Once in the comparison page, drag and drop your files to get started with the comparison process.

Each comparison has a dedicated viewer URL at /comparisons/viewer/{account_id}/{identifier} which gives users a side-by-side comparison of the documents. It displays a loading animation while the comparison is being processed. See the comparison viewer reference for more information.

Simple Example

Let's try submitting and viewing a comparison. Here's what you'll need;

  • Access to a Quelea2 Excel Compare account.
  • At least two documents of excel or csv format to compare.

Creating the Comparison

To create a comparison, log in to your account, then, from the homepage, navigate to the comparison page to gain access to the workspace.

Drag and drop your files into the fields provided and wait for the magic.

Interpreting the response

Once the comparison is processed, you'll get a workspace editor similar to the excel editor. On it, words will be highlighted to represent the common and conflicting words and phrases. You can then choose to either delete the words, merge them or edit them.

Finishing up

After choosing the actions to take on the file, you can either decide to download the resultantfile, store it, or discard it. You can also decide to store one or both of the files being compared, download one or both of the files being compoared or discard one of the files being compared.

Once discarded, they will be cleared from our database, making retrieval impossible.

Security and Authentication

All the comparisons are private by default, and require authorization to view. (You must explicitly mark them as public, if you want to grant public access.)

See the security and authentication reference for how your credentials are used, and how to display private comparisons.
